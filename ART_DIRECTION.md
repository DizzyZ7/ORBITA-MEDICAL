# ORBITA MEDICAL — Art Direction

## Direction
Soft futuristic medical minimalism.

## Visual metaphor
Healthcare is shown as an orbit: consultations, diagnostics and specialists move around one continuous patient record instead of existing as separate fragments.

## Palette
- Clinical paper: `#EDF3F1`
- Deep medical green: `#102A2A`
- Electric clinical blue: `#3067E8`
- Recovery aqua: `#77D6C8`
- Signal lime: `#D7F37C`
- Human coral: `#F18272`

## Signature interactions
- Reactive care-map object in the Hero
- Symptom-based route navigator
- Sticky connected-care orbit
- Interactive specialist selection
- Scroll-driven metrics and service reveals

The public interface does not describe the website as a concept or portfolio work.
